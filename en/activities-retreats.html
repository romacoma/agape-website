Retreats

Comprehensive recreation camps for emotional and physical recovery.

A Breath of Fresh Air

For many wheelchair users who spend most of their time within four walls, a retreat is a breath of fresh air. It is an opportunity to get out into nature, socialize, relax and receive essential support.

Every day spent in the camp brings lots of emotions: team games, themed evenings, singing by the fire, interesting workshops, and spiritual fellowship.

What the Retreat Program Includes

Recreation

Specially adapted camp territory allows smooth movement and enjoying nature.

Communication

Group discussions, themed seminars and informal socializing help find friends.

Support

Spiritual and psychological support from a team of specialists and volunteers.
