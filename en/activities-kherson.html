Trips to Kherson

Humanitarian aid and support for people in de-occupied territories.

To Save and Help

Since the beginning of the full-scale invasion, our team has actively joined in helping people who found themselves in combat zones and de-occupied territories.

In particular, we regularly carry out humanitarian missions to the Kherson region, delivering food, medicine, hygiene products, and rehabilitation equipment for those affected by the war, with special attention given to people with disabilities and the elderly.
