Who our services are for

Agape Ukraine provides physical rehabilitation services for people with disabilities resulting from spinal cord, brain injuries, as well as those who have suffered a stroke or other diseases of the central or peripheral nervous system or musculoskeletal system.

We help with:

Spinal cord injuries
Brain injuries
Effects of a stroke
Amputations
Mine-explosive trauma
Diseases of the nervous system
Consultations for children

Our Services and Methodologies

Physical Therapy

Individual sessions aimed at restoring motor functions, strength, endurance and coordination. We use modern European protocols.

Occupational Therapy

Teaching self-care skills (dressing, eating, personal hygiene). Adaptation to everyday life in new conditions.

Gym

Classes in the gym help restore muscle strength, improve coordination and endurance.

Speech Therapy

Restoration of speech and swallowing after strokes and traumatic brain injuries.

Psychological Help

Professional support for patients and their relatives in the process of accepting new life circumstances.

Spiritual Support

A team of chaplains helps to find peace of mind and new meanings for life ahead.
