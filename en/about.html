History, mission and team of Agape Ukraine

Who we are

The activities of the Agape Ukraine charitable foundation are aimed at supporting and restoring people with acquired or congenital disabilities who use wheelchairs.

Our rehabilitation center is not only a place for physical recovery, but also a space where people receive psychological and spiritual support, socialize and make new friends.

Our mission is to restore physical health and emotional balance, to help a person return to a full active life after an injury or illness.

Our Story

In 2001, a young family, Serhii and Nataliia Bolchuk from Lutsk, were involved in a car accident, as a result of which Nataliia broke her neck and remained paralyzed. A year and a half of wandering through hospitals and sanatoriums showed the family how difficult the life of people with disabilities is in Ukraine.

And the Lord prompted their hearts to help such people. They turned to the "Emmanuel" church with a request to start a ministry for people with disabilities, and the church supported them.

It seemed that the tragedy had ruined all plans for the future, but it was the tragedy that gave the impetus to start working with people with disabilities in Volyn. Over time, a team of young people was formed who were involved in this work. Initially, a team of people from the church organized holiday meetings for people with disabilities and their family members for acquaintance, communication, support, and social adaptation.

Later, the Bolchuk couple, together with the team, began organizing summer camps for people with disabilities and families raising children with disabilities. The team grew and began to cover both adults and children with disabilities and their families. Young people from the team began to receive education in the field of physical rehabilitation, nursing, social work, and manual therapy.

In 2007, a rehabilitation room was opened on the basis of the church premises, where the team provided rehabilitation and massage services on a voluntary basis. Later, the rehabilitation room turned into an active rehabilitation center.

In 2016, the Bolchuk couple, along with other key teams, founded the public organization "Agape Ukraine", on the basis of which the "Agape" Rehabilitation Complex began its activities.
