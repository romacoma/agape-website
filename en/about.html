About Us

History, mission and team of Agape Ukraine

Who we are

The activities of the Agape Ukraine charitable foundation are aimed at supporting and restoring people with acquired or congenital disabilities who use wheelchairs.

Our rehabilitation center is not only a place for physical recovery, but also a space where people receive psychological and spiritual support, socialize and make new friends.

Our mission is to restore physical health and emotional balance, to help a person return to a full active life after an injury or illness.

Our History

1999

First Steps

The history began in 1999. Then in Volyn region the first 10-day recreation camp for people with disabilities using wheelchairs was held. This camp became a catalyst for further systematic work.

2001

Mission Foundation

To improve the work and give it a legal and organized status, in 2001 the Volyn regional charitable mission was registered based on the church of Christians of Evangelical Faith.

2005

International Cooperation

The beginning of close cooperation with foreign partners. The first programs for the exchange of experience were introduced. Study trips were made to rehabilitation centers in Europe and North America.

2012

Center Design

The start of designing and fundraising for the construction of a modern fully inclusive rehabilitation center in the village of Boratyn near Lutsk.

2017

Complex Opening

The opening of the Agape Ukraine rehabilitation complex, which became the largest and most modern facility of its kind in western Ukraine. Full launch of physical and occupational therapy services.

Present Day

Scaling Operations

Expansion of activities: humanitarian trips to de-occupied territories (Kherson region), operation of a wheelchair repair workshop, regular retreats, and expansion of rehabilitation services taking into account the challenges of war.
