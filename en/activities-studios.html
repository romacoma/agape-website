Studios

Socialization, communication, and learning new skills for people with disabilities.

Space for Development

People with disabilities are often left alone with their problems, so it is crucial for us to hold meetings where they can leave their homes, communicate with each other, and share their experiences.

Our center regularly hosts studios in various directions. This is not only training but also an opportunity for wheelchair users to feel like part of society, overcome depression, and find new hobbies.

Our Directions

Computer Literacy

Training in PC usage, internet, and basic software. An opportunity to master a profession for remote work.

Cooking

An adapted equipped kitchen allows wheelchair users to cook their favorite dishes independently and learn new things.

Handicraft

Art therapy, drawing, embroidery, modeling. Helps develop fine motor skills and creativity.
